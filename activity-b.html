Code of Ethics of the Filipino Computing and Information Technology Professional

For the purposes of this Code, the following terms are defined as follows:

Information Technology

The preparation, collection, creation, transport, retrieval, storage, access, presentaion and transformation of electronic information in all its forms including but not limited to voice, graphics, text, video, data and iamge.

Information Technology Professional

One who develops or provides infromation technology products and/or services to the public.

Preamble

I will use my special knowledge and skills for the benefit of the public. I will serve emplyers and clients with integrity, subject to an overriding responsibility to the public interest, and I will strive to enhace the competence and prestige of the professional. By these, I mean:
